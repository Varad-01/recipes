Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, savory, and incredibly easy to prepare with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  3. In a pan, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Remove from heat and quickly toss in spaghetti, then stir in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water gradually to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan on top.
