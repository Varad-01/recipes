Classic Pancakes

Description

These classic pancakes are fluffy, buttery, and perfect for breakfast. Made with simple ingredients, they are easy to whip up and customize with toppings like syrup, fruits, or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Gradually combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour ¼ cup of batter for each pancake and cook until bubbles form on top. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fresh fruits.
